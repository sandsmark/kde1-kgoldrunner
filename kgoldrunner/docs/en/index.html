Introduction

KGoldrunner is a simple game where you control a green figure to collect all goldnuggets per level and escape to the top. That's very easy, but there are enemies who try to catch you. The difficulty is that you havn't any weapon to kill them directly. Your only chance is to run away or to dig a hole in the bricks where enemies can fall in. After a few seconds a captured enemy climb out of the hole, but if the hole closed befor that he dies and will be born again at his birthplace.

Control

  • walk/climb left : left arrow
  • walk/climb right : right arrow
  • climb up : up arrow
  • climb down : down arrow
  • bomb hole : ctrl or space

If you have made a level, don't forget to mail it to me
