Below is a typical game in progress. The hero (green) is at level 8 in the Tutorial game. He has 10 lives left and has scored 70,350 points (check the window's status bar). He started at the top left, dug a hole and dropped into a line of false bricks and gold where you can now see him falling. The enemy above him will fall into the hole, then climb out and be trapped up there till the end of the level. He could cause the hero some problems then, because that is where the hidden ladders appear when all the gold is gone. The hero has to use them to get to the next level. More ...

The enemy on the right is falling and carrying some gold. Notice his gold outline. The third enemy is coming down a ladder to chase the hero. The hero will have to dodge them both when he reaches the floor ... This level also shows diggable bricks and undiggable concrete and has lots of traps (false bricks), which look just like ordinary bricks, but it has no bars. Back ...
