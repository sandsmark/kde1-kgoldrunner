The KGoldrunner User's Guide

Marco Krüger and Ian Wadham
Version 1.0, January 2002

KGoldrunner is an action game where the hero runs through a maze and dodges enemies ...

You must guide the hero with the mouse and collect all the gold nuggets, then you can climb up and into the next level. The enemies also collect the gold and, worse still, try to catch and kill you!

The problem is you have no weapon to kill them. All you can do is run away, dig holes in the floor to capture them or lure them into some area where they get trapped. After a short time a captured enemy climbs out of a hole, but if the hole closes before that, he dies and will reappear somewhere else.

If you have never played before, try the "Tutorial" game, which teaches you the rules and basic skills. Then try "KGoldrunner" or "Peter W Initiation". Experts might enjoy "Wadham Family Challenge" or "Vengeance of Peter W", but "Peter W Initiation" has 100 levels and some are very difficult. It is a good game for achieving a High Score ... :-) ... As you move to more advanced levels, you will find that KGoldrunner combines action, strategy, tactics and puzzle solving --- all in one game. Good luck!
